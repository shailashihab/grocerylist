AJAX Goddess, a most dear captive in my tent He sits. I do not mean him to die yet. ATHENA Till thou hast done what, gained what further vantage? AJAX Till bound fast to a pillar beneath my roof- ATHENA What evil wilt thou inflict on the poor wretch? AJAX His back the scourge must crimson ere he dies. ATHENA Nay, do not torture so the wretched man. AJAX
